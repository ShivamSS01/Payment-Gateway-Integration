About


SSS Foundation is an NGO founded by Shivam Swastik Sahoo. This foundation has become the support system for countless number of poor families affected during the pandemic. A not-for-profit initiative aimed at fulfilling the needs of the affected people. This includes providing healthcare, education, employment to the people.



Mission

Our mission is to transform affected lives by empowering them with the necessities to lead a healthy and productive life. Helping them access better quality education, providing career opportunities, and supporting healthcare leading to self-reliance.
